Health Programs

1- Comprehensive Primary Care: Our primary care providers offer a wide range of services, including preventive care, health screenings, vaccinations, and management of chronic conditions. Whether you need a routine check-up or ongoing care, our team is here to support your overall health and well-being

2- Empowering Patients with Knowledge: Explore our web-based patient education program called Emmi. It provides easy-to-understand medical information on managing chronic conditions, preparing for procedures, and making informed health decisions. Accessible from any device, Emmi helps patients stay informed and engaged in their care

3- Long COVID Support: If you’re experiencing lingering symptoms after COVID-19, our specialized services can help. Our Centers of Excellence focus on Long COVID, providing personalized care, rehabilitation, and support for those affected. You’re not alone—we’re here to help you recover and regain your health

Services

Health Care Solutions

Cardiology

Our team of over 100 cardiologists ensures that you receive the most appropriate and advanced treatments known to medicine. We offer a full range of services, including diagnostic evaluations, non-invasive tests, invasive procedures such as cardiac catheterizations, and interventional treatments like stents, pacemaker implants, and defibrillators. Whether you need heart care or treatment, our experts are dedicated to your comfort and well-being.

Pulmonary

Our top-ranked Pulmonary Medicine Division at Mayo Clinic diagnoses and treats more than 65,000 adults and children with lung (pulmonary) and sleep disorders each year. Our team of specialists collaborates to address complex conditions affecting the lungs and breathing. Whether you need evaluation, treatment, or ongoing care, trust our experts to provide patient-centered solutions.

Neurology

a team of nationally recognized neurologists and neurosurgeons delivers comprehensive, coordinated care for conditions of the brain and nervous system. We provide care for all types of neurological disorders, from multiple sclerosis and epilepsy to stroke and neurovascular care. Your health is important to us, and we offer both in-person and virtual visits

Orthopedics

At Hospital for Special Surgery (HSS), we are proud to be consistently ranked as the top orthopedic hospital in the United States. Our team of world-class orthopedic specialists provides comprehensive care for bone, joint, and musculoskeletal conditions. From diagnosis to surgery, trust our expertise to guide your treatment options and help you move better. Whether you need spinal surgeries, joint replacements, or sports injury care, HSS is here for you.

Dental Surgery

Our team of expert dentists and oral and maxillofacial surgeons provides specialized care for a wide range of complex head, neck, and mouth conditions. Whether you need corrective jaw surgery, dental implant-based rehabilitation, treatment for sleep apnea, or facial and dental trauma care, trust us to restore function and enhance your oral health.

Laboratory

Our comprehensive test menu and assay development lab services can support your clinical trials in countries around the world. We tailor our services to meet your program’s requirements, ensuring quality, speed, and advanced diagnostics. With more than 2,250 convenient locations across the United States, we’re committed to creating a healthier world—one life at a time.
